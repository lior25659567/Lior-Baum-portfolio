## Slide 4 — type: reflection
_Words on screen: **102** · budget ~100_

- **label**  `[slides.4.label]`
  Reflection

- **title**  `[slides.4.title]`
  What I'd do differently

- **whatWorked**  `[slides.4.whatWorked.0]`
  The monochrome-grain system held the aesthetic together across every section — one committed rule, no style guide needed.

- **whatFailed**  `[slides.4.whatFailed.0]`
  I shipped on gut feeling — no scroll-speed testing, no reader feedback before launch.

- **whatYoudDoDifferently**  `[slides.4.whatYoudDoDifferently.0]`
  Test three scroll speeds with five readers before going live — pacing is the hardest thing to judge alone.

- **whatYouLearned**  `[slides.4.whatYouLearned]`
  Controlling where attention lands — through scroll timing or through a multi-step workflow — is the same design problem. That insight transfers.

- **whatYouCouldntMeasure**  `[slides.4.whatYouCouldntMeasure]`
  Whether someone unfamiliar with techno still feels the genre's energy by the end — that was the whole point, and I never measured it.
